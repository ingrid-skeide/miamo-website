About us

My name is Ingrid. I am 30 years old and last year I had a baby. Mia. Me and Mia live in Oslo in a tiny but nice apartment with my boyfriend, and Mia's dad, Sean.

Ever since Mia was born I have taken photos of her. Not just the regular photos everyone takes with their phones, but photoshoots. I wanted to do this so that Mia will have some nice photos of herself growing up.

You know when you create something nice, something you're proud of? You kinda want to show it off. I am not typically the kind of person to show off or brag about my own accomplishments. But man, Mia is just gorgeous. And I'm also kinda pleased with the photos. So that is why I am showing them off. Here in this baby blog.
